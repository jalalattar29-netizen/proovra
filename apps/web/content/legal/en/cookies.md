## 1) What Are Cookies
Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We also use similar technologies (local storage) for essential functionality.

- Analytics (optional): to understand performance and usage (only if enabled/consented where required).

## 3) Managing Cookies
You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent dashboard login.
