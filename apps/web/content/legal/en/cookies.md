# Cookie Policy – Proovra

Last Updated: 08.02.2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Proovra uses cookies and similar technologies on our websites and web-based Services.

## 1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. We may also use similar technologies like local storage, SDK identifiers, and pixels.

## 2. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

### 2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

Required for core functionality such as:

- authentication/session management,
- security protections,
- load balancing,
- basic navigation.

These cannot be disabled without breaking essential features.

### 2.2 Preferences Cookies

Used to remember choices such as:

- language settings,
- display preferences,
- region selections.

### 2.3 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Used to understand usage patterns and improve performance (e.g., page load time, errors, feature usage).  
If you offer opt-in consent banners in relevant jurisdictions, these are only enabled after consent.

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies (If Used)

Proovra may or may not use marketing cookies. If enabled, these help measure campaign effectiveness.  
If used, they will be disclosed and controlled via consent in jurisdictions where required.

## 3. HOW TO CONTROL COOKIES

You can manage cookies via:

- browser settings (block/delete),
- consent banner controls (where provided).

Note: blocking necessary cookies may prevent login or key functionality.

## 4. DO NOT TRACK

Some browsers provide “Do Not Track.” Our Services may not respond uniformly to these signals.

## 5. CHANGES

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically.
