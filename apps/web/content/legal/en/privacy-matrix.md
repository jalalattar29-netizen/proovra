# Privacy Matrix

Last Updated: 06.04.2026

This page provides a structured overview of major data categories processed in connection with Proovra.

## 1. ACCOUNT DATA

- Examples: name, email address, login provider, profile information
- Purpose: account creation, authentication, account management, support
- Legal Basis: contract, legitimate interests, legal obligations where applicable
- Retention: see Data Retention Policy

## 2. EVIDENCE DATA

- Examples: uploaded files, metadata, evidence titles, file characteristics
- Purpose: evidence capture, storage, verification, reporting, integrity workflows
- Legal Basis: customer instructions, contract, legitimate interests, legal obligations where applicable
- Retention: see Data Retention Policy

## 3. INTEGRITY ARTIFACTS

- Examples: file hashes, fingerprint hashes, signatures, timestamp metadata, custody records
- Purpose: integrity verification, tamper detection, report generation, auditability
- Legal Basis: contract, legitimate interests, customer instructions
- Retention: see Data Retention Policy

## 4. TEAM AND CASE DATA

- Examples: team membership, invites, case names, case sharing records
- Purpose: collaboration, access control, workspace organization
- Legal Basis: contract, legitimate interests

## 5. BILLING AND PAYMENT DATA

- Examples: plan information, subscription status, payment provider references
- Purpose: billing, fraud prevention, accounting, support
- Legal Basis: contract, legal obligations, legitimate interests

## 6. SECURITY AND TECHNICAL LOGS

- Examples: request metadata, IP-related security context, audit events, operational logs
- Purpose: abuse prevention, troubleshooting, incident response, security monitoring
- Legal Basis: legitimate interests, legal obligations where applicable

## 7. COOKIE AND CONSENT DATA

- Examples: cookie preference choices, consent state, legal acceptance records
- Purpose: compliance, preference management, auditability
- Legal Basis: consent, legal obligations, legitimate interests where applicable

## 8. SUPPORT COMMUNICATIONS

- Examples: support requests, legal inquiries, security reports
- Purpose: assistance, case handling, compliance, product support
- Legal Basis: contract, legitimate interests, legal obligations where applicable
