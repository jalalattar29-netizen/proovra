# Technical & Organizational Measures (TOMs)

Last Updated: 06.04.2026

This page summarizes technical and organizational measures that Proovra may implement to protect personal data, service availability, and evidence-integrity functions.

## 1. ACCESS CONTROL

Measures may include:

- role-based access restriction
- least-privilege access design
- environment-specific access control
- credential lifecycle management

## 2. AUTHENTICATION AND ACCOUNT SECURITY

Measures may include:

- authenticated access to protected services
- password security controls where applicable
- session handling controls
- provider-based authentication workflows

## 3. TRANSMISSION SECURITY

Measures may include:

- encryption in transit
- HTTPS/TLS for web traffic
- protected service-to-service communication where applicable

## 4. STORAGE SECURITY

Measures may include:

- restricted storage access
- managed database and object storage environments
- storage metadata controls for integrity workflows
- retention and object-lock features where enabled

## 5. LOGGING AND AUDITABILITY

Measures may include:

- audit logging for sensitive operations
- security and operational logging
- custody-event recording for evidence workflows
- incident investigation support logs

## 6. INTEGRITY AND CHANGE CONTROL

Measures may include:

- hashing and signature-based integrity workflows
- deployment and environment controls
- dependency and patch management
- controlled changes to security-sensitive services

## 7. AVAILABILITY AND RESILIENCE

Measures may include:

- infrastructure monitoring
- backup and recovery practices
- incident response procedures
- resilience and continuity controls appropriate to service maturity

## 8. ORGANIZATIONAL MEASURES

Measures may include:

- confidentiality obligations
- need-to-know access allocation
- vendor and subprocessor review
- policy maintenance and periodic review

## 9. IMPORTANT NOTE

Security measures evolve over time. This page is a summary and does not guarantee that every measure applies identically in every environment or service tier.
