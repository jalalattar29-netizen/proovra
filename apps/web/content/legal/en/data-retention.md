# Data Retention Policy

Last Updated: 06.04.2026

This Data Retention Policy describes how Proovra approaches retention of account data, uploaded materials, technical logs, and evidence-related integrity artifacts.

## 1. OVERVIEW

Retention periods depend on the category of data, the purpose of processing, legal obligations, security needs, and the operational need to preserve integrity verification functionality.

We aim to retain data no longer than necessary, except where longer retention is justified by legal, security, fraud-prevention, or evidentiary-integrity requirements.

## 2. ACCOUNT DATA

Account and profile data may be retained while an account remains active and for a reasonable period afterward where necessary for:

- fraud prevention
- abuse prevention
- dispute handling
- legal compliance
- security and audit purposes

## 3. EVIDENCE FILES

Evidence files uploaded or captured through the Services may be retained for as long as necessary to provide the Services and any retention period selected or applied within the product, subject to account status, legal requirements, and technical feasibility.

Deletion or account closure may affect access to evidence content, but some associated integrity records may remain retained where necessary for security, compliance, or verification continuity.

## 4. INTEGRITY ARTIFACTS

Integrity-related records may include:

- file hashes
- fingerprint hashes
- signatures
- custody event logs
- timestamp metadata
- verification reports
- related technical verification artifacts

These records may be retained longer than the underlying user-facing content where necessary to preserve the integrity and auditability of completed evidence records.

## 5. LOGS AND SECURITY RECORDS

Technical logs, audit logs, and security events may be retained for limited periods as reasonably necessary for:

- system security
- incident response
- abuse prevention
- fraud detection
- service debugging and reliability

## 6. PAYMENT AND BILLING DATA

Billing and transaction-related records may be retained as required by applicable accounting, tax, contractual, and legal obligations.

## 7. LEGAL HOLDS AND DISPUTES

Where data is subject to a legal hold, regulatory request, dispute, investigation, or similar obligation, retention may be extended as reasonably necessary.

## 8. DELETION

Where deletion is requested and legally permissible, Proovra will seek to delete or anonymize personal data within a reasonable period, subject to technical feasibility and overriding legal, security, fraud-prevention, or evidentiary-integrity requirements.
