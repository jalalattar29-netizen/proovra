# Subprocessors

Last Updated: 06.04.2026

This page identifies current third-party service providers that may process personal data or service data in connection with operation of Proovra.

The list may change over time as infrastructure, security, hosting, authentication, payment, storage, and communication services evolve.

## 1. CURRENT SUBPROCESSORS

### Cloudflare
- Purpose: CDN, DNS, edge security, network protection, TLS-related edge delivery, and performance
- Location / Transfer Scope: Global

### Hetzner
- Purpose: Infrastructure hosting and server environment
- Location / Transfer Scope: Germany / EU

### Neon
- Purpose: Managed PostgreSQL database infrastructure
- Location / Transfer Scope: EU and possibly additional regions depending on service configuration

### AWS S3
- Purpose: Object storage for evidence files, reports, and related stored materials
- Location / Transfer Scope: EU region(s), where configured

### Google
- Purpose: Google OAuth authentication and account login flows
- Location / Transfer Scope: Global

### Apple
- Purpose: Sign in with Apple authentication flows
- Location / Transfer Scope: Global

## 2. NOTES

Subprocessor use may vary depending on deployment architecture, product configuration, environment, and customer workflow.

Certain providers may act as independent controllers for their own limited processing activities.

Additional information about controller/processor roles, security measures, and contractual handling may be described in the Privacy Policy, DPA, and TOMs materials.

## 3. UPDATES

We may update this list from time to time to reflect changes in our service providers or infrastructure model.

Material changes may also be reflected in the Legal Changelog where applicable.
