## 1) Overview
This Privacy Policy explains how Proovra collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our Services.

## 2) Information We Collect
We may collect:
- Account data: name, email, authentication identifiers.
- Evidence content: photos, videos, documents you upload.
- Metadata: timestamps (UTC), device info, app version, file size, duration, MIME type, optional user-provided notes/tags.
- Optional location: only if you explicitly grant permission in-app.
- Usage and diagnostics: logs, performance metrics, crash data, security events.
- Verification and sharing events: access to verification pages, report downloads, token creation/revocation.

## 3) How We Use Information
We use information to:
- provide the Services (capture, upload, storage, verification, reports);
- secure and audit access (chain-of-custody logs);
- prevent fraud and abuse;
- improve reliability and performance;
- comply with legal obligations.

## 4) Legal Basis (GDPR)
Where GDPR applies, processing may be based on:
- contract performance (providing Services);
- legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, service improvement);
- consent (optional location, some analytics);
- legal obligations.

## 5) Sharing
We may share information:
- with service providers (hosting/storage/queues) under confidentiality and security obligations;
- with others when you create a share link/token;
- to comply with law or protect rights, safety, and security.

We do not sell personal data.

## 6) Data Retention
We retain evidence and logs as long as needed to provide the Services, comply with law, and maintain auditability. You may request deletion where legally permissible; some audit records may be retained for security and integrity.

## 7) Security
We use technical and organizational measures designed to protect information, including signed URLs, access controls, and immutable audit logs. No system is 100% secure; you are responsible for protecting your account and share links.

## 8) International Transfers
If data is processed outside your country, we take steps to provide appropriate safeguards as required by law.

## 9) Your Rights
