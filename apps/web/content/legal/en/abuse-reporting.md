# Abuse & Unlawful Content Reporting

Last Updated: 06.04.2026

This page explains how to report misuse of PROOVRA, unlawful content, or platform abuse.

It is intended to provide a clear reporting route for misuse involving evidence workflows, verification features, or unlawful platform activity.

## 1. OVERVIEW

Proovra prohibits illegal, abusive, fraudulent, or harmful use of the Services.

We may review reports relating to:

- illegal content
- privacy violations
- harassment or unlawful surveillance
- abuse of verification or evidence features
- fraudulent or misleading use of evidence records
- malware, account abuse, or platform attacks

## 2. HOW TO REPORT

Please send reports to:

support@proovra.com

Where relevant, include:

- the relevant URL, verification link, or evidence identifier
- a description of the issue
- why you believe the content or activity is unlawful or abusive
- supporting screenshots or reference materials where appropriate
- your contact details if you want a follow-up

## 3. REVIEW PROCESS

We may assess reports and, where appropriate, take actions such as:

- requesting more information
- restricting access
- removing or disabling content
- suspending or terminating accounts
- preserving records for investigation
- reporting to competent authorities where legally required

## 4. IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS

Proovra does not routinely pre-screen all user content.

We review reports based on available information, legal obligations, and operational capacity.

## 5. FALSE OR ABUSIVE REPORTS

You must not knowingly submit false, misleading, or abusive reports.
