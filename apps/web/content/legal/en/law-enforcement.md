# Law Enforcement Request Policy

Last Updated: 06.04.2026

This policy describes how Proovra responds to requests from law enforcement agencies, regulators, courts, and other governmental authorities.

## 1. GENERAL PRINCIPLES

Proovra respects user rights, applicable law, and due process.

We disclose user data only where we determine that disclosure is legally required or otherwise permitted under applicable law.

## 2. VALID LEGAL PROCESS

We may respond to:

- court orders
- subpoenas
- legally binding governmental requests
- emergency requests where legally permissible
- other lawful demands from competent authorities

Requests must be appropriately scoped and issued by competent bodies.

## 3. TYPES OF INFORMATION THAT MAY BE AVAILABLE

Depending on the account, workflow, and retention state, Proovra may hold information such as:

- account registration data
- service usage metadata
- logs and access records
- evidence-related technical verification data
- billing-related information
- support communications

We do not guarantee that any particular category of data exists, is retained, or is available in a given case.

## 4. EVIDENCE CONTENT

Proovra provides technical integrity and verification tooling. We do not independently interpret, endorse, or certify the factual content of user-uploaded materials.

## 5. EMERGENCY REQUESTS

In limited cases involving imminent risk of death, serious physical harm, or similar emergency situations, Proovra may assess emergency disclosure requests as permitted by applicable law.

## 6. USER NOTICE

Where legally permitted and appropriate, Proovra may notify affected users before disclosure.
