# Law Enforcement Request Policy

Last Updated: 08.02.2026

This policy describes how Proovra responds to requests from law enforcement agencies and governmental authorities.

## 1. General Principles

Proovra respects user privacy and the rule of law.

We disclose user data only when legally required and only through valid legal processes.

## 2. Legal Requirements

Proovra may disclose information in response to:

- court orders
- subpoenas
- legally binding government requests
- other lawful investigative demands

Requests must be issued by competent authorities.

## 3. Scope of Disclosure

Where disclosure is legally required, Proovra may provide:

- account registration information
- service usage metadata
- evidence verification data
- logs required for investigation

Proovra does not guarantee that specific data will exist or be retained.

## 4. Evidence Content

User-uploaded evidence remains under the control of the user.

Proovra does not interpret, validate, or certify evidence content.

Our system only records cryptographic integrity information.

## 5. Emergency Requests

In limited circumstances involving imminent risk of harm, Proovra may evaluate emergency disclosure requests in accordance with applicable law.

## 6. Transparency

Where legally permitted, Proovra may notify affected users before disclosing their information.

## 7. Requests Contact

Law enforcement authorities may submit requests to:

legal@proovra.com
