# Legal Content

Legal content is stored as Markdown per locale:
- `apps/web/content/legal/en/*`
- `apps/web/content/legal/ar/*`
- `apps/web/content/legal/de/*`

Notes:
- Arabic and German are machine-assisted and require legal review.
- `/legal/[slug]` supports privacy|terms|cookies|security.
- Manual language switcher is visible on legal pages.
